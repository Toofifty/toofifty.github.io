Hi!

I'm Alex Matheson, an aspiring programmer originating from Ballarat, Victoria. I make games, apps, websites, scripts, animations -- whatever I feel like mostly. If you want to know more about me and my skills, check the about tab.

While you're here, check out the projects I've got going on in the experiments tab, and play around with some in the playground.

There's some external links at the bottom of the page you could check out too, if that's what you're into.

Cheers!

about

Mainly self-taught while in high school, programming seemed like a nifty thing to do in my spare time. So now I'm studying for a Bachelor of Software Engineering at RMIT University, Melbourne.

These are the skills I have so far:

  • Extensive knowledge of application development languages Python, Java, C# and Lua.
  • Skills in web development; HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and PHP.
  • Skills in Git and its usage.
  • Image manipulation skills in tools such as: Paint.NET, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Flash and Adobe Illustrator.
  • Experience with Polymer, Bootstrap, Three.js, Cordova and Crosswalk frameworks.
